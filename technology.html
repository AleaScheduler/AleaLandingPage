How It Works

Unlike deterministic algorithms, Alea uses controlled randomness to explore millions of possibilities.

1

Probabilistic Construction

The engine calculates costs (Load + Rules + Proximity) and adds random noise to candidate scores. This allows it to escape local traps and generate diverse valid schedules.

2

Iterative Optimization

Once a valid schedule is found, the engine enters a deterministic rebalancing phase. It smooths out workload distribution via intelligent swaps to reach an optimal state.
